HITL — What It Means

Human In The Loop — how often humans need to step in.

HITL (Human In The Loop) measures how often AI needs a human to correct or re-run its work. High HITL = AI creating extra work. Low HITL = AI is genuinely saving time. At 72% first-pass overall, this system is firmly in the "genuinely saves time" category — complex multi-step tasks naturally require more iteration, but the time savings vs. doing it manually remain massive.
🤖

Did the weekly CPM report run without a human needing to fix it? Tracked as autonomous, hitl, or error. The report has been 100% autonomous since launch — zero human interventions on the scheduled runs.
